# The boardroom demo

Ninety seconds, five features, zero slides. Run on a phone, installed to the
home screen, demo seed loaded (`pnpm seed:demo`).

## The script

1. **Open Today.** The date, four presence dots, one call to make. Press
   play and let the morning read run for twenty seconds. Point out the text
   twin one tap away.
2. **Swipe two Radar cards.** Databricks CustomerLake first: lane chip,
   two sources, the "for Amperity" line, the move. Swipe. Every card cited
   or it does not render.
3. **Tap the assumption chip.** The Ledger opens on "Identity is the
   substrate AI runs on": confidence 72, down 8 on the quarter, the evidence
   list showing exactly why. The house view is a living thing.
4. **Open Table.** The alignment plot: one dot high and wide. "Widest split
   this week: the lakehouse partner motion, Amy at 40, Derek at 85. Worth ten
   minutes on Monday." Nobody had to run that meeting to find that split.
5. **Hold to talk in Ask** (from G3): "Log a decision: we run the lakehouse
   partner review this quarter, Derek owns it." Confirm card appears, done.
   Until G3 ships, log the same decision typed from the Table room.

## The close

The sample data comes out, your priorities go in, and this is live for the
four of us tomorrow morning.

## Reset

`pnpm seed:demo` restores the exact demo state. `pnpm seed:live` empties it
for real use.
